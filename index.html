Research

  • Unsecured Lending via Delegated Underwriting: enables unsecured lending without collateral, identity checks, or centralized underwriting
  • Commitment-Satisfaction Committees: introduces an in-protocol mechanism where attester subcommittees enforce arbitrary proposer commitments under ePBS (solves Ethereum Foundation's open problem ROP-4)
  • SOLO: Liquid Staking for Solo Validators: lowers the 32 ETH requirement for solo Ethereum validators to 1.25 ETH
  • zkUniswap: offloads swap computation to a zk coprocessor, verifies proofs on-chain, and captures MEV through pool-lock auctions
  • PEPC-DVT: enforces proposer commitments through distributed validator technology with no Ethereum consensus changes
  • Making PEPC-DVT Private: uses BLS blinded multi-signatures to enforce private proposer commitments
  • Consensus Algorithms for Blockchains: peer-reviewed survey of proof-of-work, proof-of-stake, and delegated proof-of-stake, with a blockchain implementation
