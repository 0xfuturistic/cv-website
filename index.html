Research

  • Unsecured Lending via Delegated Underwriting: no collateral, no identity, no centralized underwriting. Existing users sponsor new borrowers by putting their own credit on the line, making fake accounts worthless by construction
  • SOLO: Liquid Staking for Solo Validators: a solo validator on Ethereum needs 32 ETH today. SOLO reduces that to 1.25 ETH through a permissionless LST minted against the portion of stake that can't be lost to slashing
  • zkUniswap: offloads UniswapV3 swap computation to a zk co-processor with on-chain proof verification, and captures MEV by auctioning pool locks
  • Commitment-Satisfaction Committees: solved Ethereum Foundation's open problem ROP-4, an in-protocol mechanism where attester subcommittees enforce arbitrary proposer commitments under ePBS
  • PEPC-DVT: proposer commitments enforced through distributed validator technology, with no changes to Ethereum consensus. One of the first out-of-protocol PEPC solutions
  • Making PEPC-DVT private: BLS blinded multi-signatures let the DVT network sign blocks without seeing their contents, enabling a PEPCified MEV-boost
  • Consensus Algorithms for Blockchains: peer-reviewed survey of PoW, PoS, and DPoS with a from-scratch implementation, published in Olympiads in Informatics
