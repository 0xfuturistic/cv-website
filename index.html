Research

  • Delegated Underwriting: decentralized lending without collateral, identity checks, or centralized underwriting
  • Commitment-Satisfaction Committees: attester subcommittees that enforce arbitrary proposer commitments under ePBS (solves the Ethereum Foundation's open problem ROP-4)
  • SOLO: lowers the solo validator entry barrier from 32 ETH to 1.25 ETH
  • zkUniswap: offloads swap computation to a zk coprocessor, verifies proofs on-chain, and captures MEV through pool-lock auctions
  • PEPC-DVT: enforces proposer commitments through distributed validator technology without consensus changes
  • Private PEPC-DVT: private proposer commitments via BLS blinded multi-signatures
  • Consensus Algorithms for Blockchains: peer-reviewed survey of proof-of-work, proof-of-stake, and delegated proof-of-stake, with a blockchain implementation
